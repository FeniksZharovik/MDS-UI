F-117 Nighthawk

The F-117 is the world's first stealth aircraft, developed by Lockheed Martin for the U.S. Air Force. Designed to evade radar detection, it features a distinctive angular shape and advanced stealth technology. While primarily intended for ground-attack missions, the F-117 was retired from active service in 2008.

Su-34 Fullback

The Su-34 is a Russian multirole fighter-bomber designed for air-to-ground combat. It boasts long-range capabilities, heavy payload capacity, and robust crew protection, including an armored cockpit. With its delta-wing design and twin engines, the Su-34 excels in both combat and patrol missions.

PzH 2000

The Panzerhaubitze 2000 is a highly advanced German self-propelled artillery system. Equipped with a 155mm cannon, it delivers exceptional range and accuracy, capable of firing multiple rounds in rapid succession. The PzH 2000 is renowned for its precision, rate of fire, and durability in combat scenarios.
